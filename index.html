Explore Kashmir

Discover the breathtaking beauty of Kashmir through our gallery of stunning locations.

Dal Lake

The jewel in the crown of Kashmir, famous for its houseboats and floating gardens.

Gulmarg

A premier ski destination with the world's highest gondola ride.

Pahalgam

The Valley of Shepherds with stunning landscapes and river views.

Sonamarg

The Meadow of Gold, known for its scenic beauty and glaciers.

Mughal Gardens

Historic gardens built by Mughal emperors with terraced lawns and cascading fountains.

Tulip Garden

Asia's largest tulip garden with over 1.5 million tulips of various colors.
